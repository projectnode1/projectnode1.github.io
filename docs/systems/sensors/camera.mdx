# Camera

Camera has a long history and in its simplest form is a device that captures light
and records it as an image.

Think of a pinhole camera as the simplest form of it where the light from different objects
in the scene passes through a small hole and forms an image on the opposite side.

It forms an inverted image because light travels in straight lines through a small aperture.
One may ask why light travel in straight line?
